📖 About JUSTICE-KP

JUSTICE-KP is a neuro-symbolic AI system assisting Barangay officials in processing disputes under the Katarungang Pambarangay framework (RA 7160). It combines NLP, forward-chaining expert systems, and Explainable AI for consistent, compliant case handling.

🔄 Case Workflow (8-Step Process)

1. Consent Form — RA 4200 all-party consent verified.
2. Complaint Filing — Parties and dispute type entered.
3. Decision Branch — Blotter-only vs Summons path chosen.
4. Scheduling — Hearing scheduled; summons generated.
5. Hearing Consent — Second consent for hearing recording.
6. NER + Emotion Analysis — AI extracts legal facts and emotional context.
7. Expert System — 50 RA 7160 rules evaluated; XAI trace shown.
8. Mediation Output — Strategies and KP forms auto-generated.

⚠ VAWC / Sensitive Cases

Cases flagged with VAWC or other sensitive indicators are automatically routed to the Pink Blotter path and generate a BPO referral. These bypass normal mediation per RA 9262. Trigger keywords are detected automatically during NER extraction.
